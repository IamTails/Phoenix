Follow Streets


1. Head over to https://console.developers.google.com/ and sign in with your Google account.
2. Click on project (nearby Google APIs logo) and click Create Project.
3. Now click "Library" under API Manager. You will see a list of Popular APIs.
4. Look at the Google Maps APIs and click "More" and Click "Google Maps Directions API". Then hit "Enable".
5. Now, go back to Library again and click on "Google Maps Elevations API" and hit Enable.
6. Now, click "Credentials" under API Manager and click "Create Credentials". Then select "API Key".
7. Your Google API key will be shown and you can copy paste it in the config file now.
8. Choose Google for follow streets. "followStreets" : [ "GOOGLE" ]
